# Outreach Playbook: InMail Strategy & Rules

InMails are premium messages that allow you to reach out directly to 2nd and 3rd-degree connections without sending a connection request first. To maximize InMail ROI and protect precious credits, outreach must be highly strategic and data-driven.

## InMail Performance Benchmarks (The Ground Truth)

LinkedIn's official global talent and sales data reveals critical benchmarks for InMail response rates:

1.  **Brevity is King:**
    *   **Under 400 characters:** Receives a **22% higher response rate** than the global average.
    *   **Over 1,200 characters:** Performs **11% below average**.
    *   *Strategic Takeaway:* Draft short, punchy messages focused on a single call-to-action (CTA).
2.  **Individual Personalization (+15% Lift):**
    *   Individually drafted and personalized InMails perform **15% better** than bulk-sent templates.
3.  **The Pre-Research View (+78% Lift):**
    *   Viewing a prospect's profile within 30 days before sending an InMail makes them **78% more likely to accept it**.
    *   Following the prospect's company page first makes them **15% more likely to accept**.
    *   If the prospect already follows *your* company page, they are **270% more likely to accept**.
4.  **Timing & Schedule Warnings:**
    *   **Avoid Saturdays (-8% response)** and Fridays (-4% response).
    *   Monday mornings are the absolute best time to send InMails. Sunday to Thursday messages perform consistently near the average.
5.  **Target High-Intent Profiles (+35-37% Lift):**
    *   "Recommended Matches" are **35% more likely** to respond.
    *   "Open to Work" candidates are **37% more likely** to respond.

## The Perfect InMail Framework (Under 400 Characters)

To capture the +22% lift, messages must be concise, personalized, and have a soft call to action.

### Under 400 Characters Template (B2B Sourcing / Sales)

```text
Subject: [Uncommon Commonalities] + [Quick Question]

Hi [Name], saw your recent post on [Topic]—loved your take on [Specific Point]. 

I notice you're scaling the team at [Company]. We recently helped [Similar Company] resolve [Pain Point] with [Our Solution]. 

Are you open to a brief chat next week to see if it aligns with your Q3 goals?

Best,
[Name]
```

*Character Analysis:*
*   Subject line: 35 characters
*   Body text: 280 characters
*   Total: ~315 characters (Perfectly under the 400-character threshold for maximum response rate).

## InMail Credit Economics

*   **Refund Policy:** LinkedIn refunds your InMail credit if the recipient responds (either accepting or declining) within 90 days. This means hyper-targeted, high-quality messages that guarantee a response (even a polite "No") are essentially free, while ignored spam costs credits.
*   **Active Log Maintenance:** Monitor pending InMails and response rates to optimize templates and adjust audience targeting parameters.
