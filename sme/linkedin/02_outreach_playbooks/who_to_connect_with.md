# Outreach Playbook: Who to Connect With (Target Sourcing)

Target sourcing is the process of defining, locating, and selecting the highest-potential profiles on LinkedIn to connect with or message. To maximize efficiency and conversion, target sourcing must be data-backed and highly systematic.

## Sourcing Prioritization Matrix

SDRs, recruiters, and founders must prioritize prospects based on intent and activity signals:

| Priority | Sourcing Persona / Signal | Why Target Them | Sourcing Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1 (High Intent)** | **New Role (Last 90 Days)** | **62% more likely to accept**; eager to make changes and bring in new solutions/vendors. | Sourcing via Sales Navigator's "Changed jobs in past 90 days" spotlight filter. |
| **Tier 1 (High Intent)** | **Page Followers** | **270% more likely to respond**; already know and trust your company's brand. | Sourcing company page followers matching target buyer personas. |
| **Tier 1 (High Intent)** | **Recommended Matches** | **35% higher response rate**; pre-vetted by LinkedIn's sequence matching algorithm. | Sourcing via Recruiter/Sales Navigator recommendations lists. |
| **Tier 2 (Active)** | **Posted in Last 30 Days** | **45% more likely to accept**; active on the platform and highly engaged with content. | Sourcing via the "Posted on LinkedIn in past 30 days" spotlight filter. |
| **Tier 2 (Active)** | **Open to Work (Recruiters)** | **37% higher response rate**; actively looking for jobs or new business. | Sourcing via the Open to Work spotlight filter. |
| **Tier 3 (Passive)** | **Passive Buyer/Candidate** | Large high-quality talent/lead pool, but requires deep research and warm paths. | Sourcing via core keyword searches combined with warm path matching. |

## Defining Target Personas

To prevent outbound "spray and pray," define clear buyer or candidate personas:

### 1. The B2B Buyer Persona (Sales Prospecting)
*   **Key Titles:** VP of Sales, Chief Technology Officer, Head of Product.
*   **Company Size:** 50-200 employees (mid-market) or 1000+ (enterprise).
*   **Geography:** Targeted based on regional focus (e.g., North America, Western Europe).
*   **Department Headcount Growth:** Focus on companies with over 10-15% growth over the past year.

### 2. The Tech Candidate Persona (Sourcing / Recruiting)
*   **Key Titles:** Senior Software Engineer, Principal Devops Engineer, ML Researcher.
*   **Core Skills:** Must explicitly list target skills (e.g., Python, Kubernetes, PyTorch).
*   **Years of Experience:** 5-10 years in similar roles.

---

## Actionable Sourcing Playbook for Agents

1.  **Run Structured Search Queries:** Construct Boolean search queries matching the target persona.
2.  **Apply Spotlights:** Filter candidates using `Changed Jobs`, `Posted on LinkedIn`, or `Share experiences` to isolate Tier 1 and Tier 2 prospects.
3.  **Cross-Reference Profile Completion:** Prioritize prospects with complete, professional profiles, as they are highly active on the platform.
4.  **Save as Lead:** Always save target prospects to a dedicated list in Sales Navigator or Recruiter to keep track of their updates and timing signals.
