# User Intent: Job Search

The "Job Seeker" intent represents members actively or passively searching for new career opportunities. Sourcing, networking, and application behaviors are distinct and predictable for this persona.

## Behavioral Signals of a Job Seeker

1.  **Direct Signaling:**
    *   Toggling "Open to Work" status (either visible publicly or restricted to recruiters).
    *   Updating profile sections (Headline, Experience, Skills) and seeking recommendations.
2.  **Platform Interaction Patterns:**
    *   Subscribing to Job Alerts.
    *   Following target company pages and employee profiles (especially hiring managers).
    *   Engaging with "We're Hiring" posts via comments or likes.

## Strategic Playbook for the Job Seeker Persona

### 1. Sourcing Targets (Where to look)
*   **Company Map:** Create a target company list of 30-50 organizations.
*   **Employee Directory:** Search target companies for "Talent Acquisition", "Recruiting", and "Hiring Manager" titles.
*   **Alumni Network:** Leverage the company page or University search to find alumni working at target companies (ideal "warm path").

### 2. Sourcing Strategy (The Warm Outreach)
*   Instead of cold applying, seek an internal referral. An internal referral increases landing an interview by 10x.
*   **Outreach Playbook:**
    *   *Step 1:* View the hiring manager or internal contact's profile (registers as a profile view).
    *   *Step 2:* Wait 1 day. Send a personalized invitation (max 200/300 chars) expressing interest in their team's work, not begging for a job.
    *   *Outreach Template:*
        ```text
        "Hi [Name], saw your team's recent launch of [Product]—incredible work. I'm a [Role] with experience in [Skill], and I've been following [Company] closely. Would love to connect and keep in touch!"
        ```

### 3. Application Optimization
*   Tailor the resume and profile keywords to match the specific job description.
*   Leverage "Easy Apply" for high-volume, but prioritize high-value applications through direct messaging and warm paths.
