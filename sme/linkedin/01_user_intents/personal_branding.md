# User Intent: Personal Branding

The "Personal Branding" intent represents individuals seeking to establish themselves as industry experts, thought leaders, and trusted voices in their professional communities.

## Core Objectives of Personal Branding

1.  **Inbound Opportunities:** Attract job offers, consulting gigs, speaking invitations, and potential clients organically without outbound pitching.
2.  **Authority & Credibility:** Elevate corporate profile, build trust for their company's brand, and build a dedicated follower base.
3.  **Industry Networking:** Connect with peers, high-profile influencers, and prospective partners.

## Personal Branding Playbook

### 1. Profile Optimization as a Creator
*   **Creator Mode:** Enable Creator Mode to turn "Follow" into the primary action and display featured hashtags.
*   **Featured Section:** Curate your best-performing posts, articles, newsletter registrations, or personal portfolios. Keep this section updated with high-impact links.
*   **Detailed Experience:** Highlight unique domain achievements, leadership philosophy, and industry impact.

### 2. Content Creation Framework
To build a sustainable personal brand, creators rely on structured **content pillars**:
*   **Pillar 1: Deep Expertise:** Case studies, step-by-step technical guides, and problem-solving breakdowns.
*   **Pillar 2: Personal Narrative:** Behind-the-scenes struggles, lessons learned from failure, and personal growth stories.
*   **Pillar 3: Industry Trends:** Curation of breaking news, thoughtful opinions on market direction, and future predictions.

### 3. Engagement Strategy (The 80/20 Rule)
A brand isn't built solely by posting. Interaction is critical:
*   **Post and Engage:** Spend 15 minutes before and 30 minutes after posting commenting on other creators' posts in the same industry.
*   **Reply to Every Comment:** The algorithm highly values active discussion under your posts. Always respond to comments with thoughtful replies that keep the conversation going.
*   **Leave Thoughtful Comments:** Write detailed, expert comments on industry leaders' posts. This acts as a "mini-post" that drives massive profile visits from their audience.
