# User Intent: Recruiting (Sourcing Strategy)

The "Recruiting" intent represents professional recruiters and talent acquisition specialists sourcing, engaging, and securing passive and active candidates for open positions.

##Sourcing Funnel & candidate Engagement

### 1. Candidate Sourcing Strategy
*   **Sourcing Strings:** Build highly specific Boolean search strings using operators (`AND`, `OR`, `NOT`, `""`, `()`) to filter out irrelevant candidates.
*   **Targeting Spotlights:** Sourcing strategies should prioritize candidates with higher engagement probabilities:
    *   **Open to Work:** **37% higher response rate**. Represent immediate hiring potential.
    *   **Recommended Matches:** Up to **35% higher response rate**. These are AI-suggested candidates based on successful placements.
    *   **Company Connections & Followers:** High affinity candidates who already know the brand.

### 2. Crafting the Perfect Outreach Message
Based on extensive LinkedIn data, recruitment outreach should follow strict structural and behavioral guidelines to maximize candidate response rates:

*   **Brevity is Critical:**
    *   Messages under **400 characters** receive a **22% higher response rate** than average.
    *   Messages over 1,200 characters perform **11% below average**.
    *   *Rule of Thumb:* Keep it under 1-2 paragraphs, stating the role, why their profile stands out, and a soft CTA.
*   **Hyper-Personalization (+15% Lift):**
    *   Avoid bulk templates. Mention a specific project, skill, or former company from their profile.
    *   *"Whatever stands out to you at first glance, that's what you talk about in your outreach. Show them that you actually read their profile."*
*   **Schedule Wisely:**
    *   Monday is the absolute best day to send a message.
    *   **Strictly avoid Saturdays (-8% response)** and Fridays (-4% response). Schedule Friday outreach to send on Monday morning.

### 3. Sourcing Metrics & Feedback Loop
To constantly improve response rates, recruiters must track:
*   **InMail Response Rate:** A critical KPI because **LinkedIn refunds InMail credits for any message that receives a response within 90 days** (even if the candidate declines). This makes efficient outreach highly cost-effective.
*   **Sourcing Funnel Conversion:** From Search Results -> Profile Clicks -> Message Sent -> Response Received -> Initial Call Booked.
*   **Lesson Logging:** Keep an active log of which messaging templates and value propositions work best for specific technical or business personas.
