# Posting & Content Formats

LinkedIn supports a variety of content formats on its platform. Choosing the right format depends heavily on the specific target audience, topic complexity, and goals (e.g., awareness, discussion, click-throughs).

## Core Content Formats

### 1. Text-Only Posts
*   **Description:** Short or long-form written text without media.
*   **Character Limit:** Up to 3,000 characters.
*   **Best Uses:** Personal stories, opinions, industry takeaways, and short updates.
*   **Key Strategies:** Use spacing, bullet points, and strong hooks in the first 3 lines before the "See more" truncation.

### 2. Single or Multiple Photos / Images
*   **Description:** Visual posts featuring one or more images.
*   **Supported Formats:** JPG, PNG, WebP.
*   **Best Uses:** Event photos, company updates, team spotlights, or simple visual quote cards.
*   **Key Strategies:** Alt-text should be configured for accessibility and SEO. Multiple images display as a gallery grid, which increases visual interest.

### 3. Video Posts
*   **Description:** Native video uploads.
*   **Supported Formats:** MP4, WebM.
*   **Best Uses:** Behind-the-scenes footage, executive announcements, short tutorials, and product demonstrations.
*   **Key Strategies:** Keep videos under 2 minutes. **Always add closed captions/subtitles**, as over 80% of members view videos on mute in their feeds.

### 4. Document Posts (PDF Carousels)
*   **Description:** PDFs uploaded to be viewed as slide-by-slide carousels directly within the feed.
*   **Best Uses:** Educational step-by-step guides, lists, cheat sheets, and report summaries.
*   **Key Strategies:** This is one of the highest-performing content formats for organic reach and engagement. Design with large, readable fonts and visual transitions.

### 5. Polls
*   **Description:** Simple single-question surveys with up to 4 multiple-choice options.
*   **Duration Options:** 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, or 2 weeks.
*   **Best Uses:** Market research, audience engagement, and quick opinion-gathering.
*   **Key Strategies:** Keep questions controversial or highly relatable to maximize participation. Follow up with poll participants via messaging to build relationships.

### 6. Articles & Newsletters
*   **Description:** Long-form publishing platform (similar to blog posts) with rich text editing.
*   **Best Uses:** In-depth thought leadership, industry overviews, and structured educational pieces.
*   **Key Strategies:** Articles are indexed by external search engines (Google), boosting off-platform organic discovery. Newsletters allow members to subscribe to receive regular updates.

### 7. Link Posts
*   **Description:** Text posts containing an external URL that displays a preview card (thumbnail, title, description).
*   **Strategic Warning:** LinkedIn's feed algorithm historically downranks posts containing external links to keep users on the platform.
*   **Workaround:** Put the external link in the comments section and mention "link in comments" in the post text, or edit the link into the post after publishing.
