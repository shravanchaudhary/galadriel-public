# Jobs & Recruiting

LinkedIn serves as the premier global platform for professional recruitment, job search, and employer branding. It acts as a bridge between active/passive talent and hiring organizations.

## Core Concepts & Interface Surfaces

### 1. The Job Seeker Journey
*   **Job Search Filters:** Searchable by title, keyword, location, remote/hybrid/on-site, company, experience level, job function, and salary.
*   **Easy Apply:** Allows candidates to apply in seconds using their LinkedIn profile details and an optional resume PDF.
*   **Open to Work Badge:**
    *   **Public:** Displays a green `#OpenToWork` photo frame visible to all members.
    *   **Recruiters Only:** Only visible to users with a LinkedIn Recruiter subscription.
    *   **Impact:** Candidates who signal "Open to Work" see a **37% higher InMail response rate** from recruiters than passive candidates.

### 2. The Recruiter Dashboard (LinkedIn Recruiter)
*   A premium enterprise workspace used by corporate and agency recruiters to source talent.
*   **Key Sourcing Filters:**
    *   Current/Past Title, Current/Past Company, Skills, Location, Year of Graduation.
    *   **Spotlights:** Advanced filters targeting candidates who are:
        *   "Open to work"
        *   "Have company connections"
        *   "Follow your company page" (extremely high intent)
        *   "More likely to respond" (based on past platform activity)
*   **Recommended Matches:** LinkedIn's AI-generated candidate suggestions. Contacting "Recommended Matches" results in up to a **35% higher acceptance rate** compared to cold search sourcing.

### 3. Strategic Sourcing Insights
*   **Passive Sourcing:** Sourcing candidates who are not actively looking (no "Open to work" badge) but are highly qualified. Requires hyper-personalized outreach.
*   **Active Sourcing:** Sourcing candidates with "Open to work" signals. Response rates are higher, and hiring cycles are shorter.
*   **The Sourcing Funnel:**
    1.  *Search:* Query string construction utilizing Boolean operators.
    2.  *Filter:* Apply Spotlights and location filters.
    3.  *Research:* Review target profiles for "uncommon commonalities".
    4.  *Outreach:* Send personalized InMails or connection requests (avoiding Fridays and Saturdays).
